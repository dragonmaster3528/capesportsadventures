Our Story

Cape Sports Adventures was born from a simple idea: that the best way to experience a place is through its passions. And in South Africa, few things ignite passion like sport.

Founded in 2015 by a team of Cape Town locals with backgrounds in sports management, hospitality, and adventure tourism, we set out to create something different from the typical tour operator. We wanted to build experiences that go beyond the stadium—trips that immerse visitors in the culture, landscapes, and spirit that make the Western Cape one of the most extraordinary destinations on Earth.

What started as rugby tour packages for visiting supporters has grown into a full-service travel company specialising in sports tourism and outdoor adventure. Today, we help thousands of travellers each year—from solo adventurers to corporate groups, school teams to golfing buddies—discover their own Cape Town story.

Why Cape Town?

There's nowhere else quite like it. Where else can you watch world-class rugby in the shadow of Table Mountain, play golf along dramatic clifftops, dive with great white sharks before lunch, and taste award-winning wines at sunset—all in the same weekend?

The Western Cape offers an unmatched concentration of experiences: championship golf courses that rival anywhere in the world, a rugby culture as passionate as any in the Six Nations or Rugby Championship, adventure activities from paragliding to whale watching, and a food and wine scene that's earned global recognition.

Add to that year-round mild weather, exceptional value for international visitors, world-class infrastructure, and some of the most welcoming people you'll ever meet—and you have the perfect destination for the trip of a lifetime.

Our Expertise

Sports Tours

Rugby, cricket, football, hockey, netball—we organise touring packages for amateur and school teams, including fixtures against local clubs, professional coaching clinics, and behind-the-scenes access to world-class training facilities.

Fan Experiences

Watch the Springboks at Cape Town Stadium, experience the electric atmosphere of the Cape Town Sevens, or catch the Proteas in action at Newlands. We secure tickets, arrange hospitality, and build complete match-day packages.

Golf

The Western Cape is home to some of Africa's finest courses. From the legendary Links at Fancourt to the clifftop drama of Pinnacle Point, we create golf experiences that combine world-class play with stunning scenery.

Adventure

Shark cage diving in Gansbaai, paragliding from Lion's Head, abseiling off Table Mountain, whale watching in Hermanus—the Western Cape is an adventure playground, and we know it inside out.

Corporate & Groups

Team-building retreats, incentive travel, conference add-ons—we design group experiences that combine business objectives with unforgettable Cape Town moments.

Winelands & Culture

No visit to the Cape is complete without exploring the Winelands. We curate wine tours, culinary experiences, and cultural excursions that reveal the depth and diversity of this region.
