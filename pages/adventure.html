Gansbaai

Come face-to-face with the ocean's most feared predator in the crystal-clear waters off Gansbaai. This once-in-a-lifetime experience offers the ultimate adrenaline rush in complete safety.

  • Safety briefing and equipment provision

Table Mountain Hiking

Table Mountain National Park

Conquer Cape Town's iconic flat-topped mountain via the famous Platteklip Gorge route or take the more adventurous India Venster route.

Route Options:

  • Platteklip Gorge: Classic route, moderate difficulty
  • India Venster: Scrambling and chains, challenging
  • Lion's Head: Sunrise/sunset hike with chains
  • Skeleton Gorge: Forested route via Kirstenbosch
4-6 Hours Moderate-Hard From R650

Safety & Requirements

Safety Standards

  • Certified and experienced guides
  • Regular equipment safety checks
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage
  • Emergency procedures and first aid
  • Weather contingency plans
  • Small group sizes for personalized attention

What to Bring

  • Comfortable hiking/sports shoes
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Sun protection (hat, sunglasses, sunscreen)
  • Water bottle and snacks
  • Camera for memorable moments
  • Sense of adventure and positive attitude!

Fitness Requirements

  • Basic fitness level for hiking activities
  • No swimming required for shark cage diving
  • Age restrictions apply to some activities
  • Medical conditions must be disclosed
  • Weight limits for paragliding and helicopter flights
  • Alternative activities available if needed
