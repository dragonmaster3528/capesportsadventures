Booking Information

How to Book

  1. Contact Us: Get in touch with your preferred dates and match interests
  2. Consultation: We'll discuss your requirements and customize your package
  3. Confirmation: Receive detailed itinerary and secure your booking
  4. Final Details: Complete arrangements 48 hours before your experience

What's Included

  • Match tickets (category depends on package)
  • Pre-match hospitality and catering
  • Professional tour guide services
  • Transport to/from venues
  • Commemorative merchandise
  • Emergency support during events

Booking Policies

  • Advance Booking: Recommended 4-6 weeks for major matches
  • Payment Terms: 50% deposit required, balance due 14 days prior
  • Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled 21+ days before event
  • Weather Policy: Indoor alternatives provided for outdoor events
  • Group Sizes: Minimum 2 people, maximum varies by venue
  • Age Restrictions: Some packages have minimum age requirements

Special Requirements

We accommodate dietary restrictions, accessibility needs, and special celebrations. Please inform us during booking of any requirements.
